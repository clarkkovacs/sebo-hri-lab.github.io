HRI Lab Meeting Info

HRI Lab Meeting Time & Place

For this quarter (Fall 2021) the HRI lab meetings will take place every Monday from 4:00pm - 5:00pm in JCL 354. Our first meeting of the quarter is on Oct 4, 2021.


Lab Meeting Schedule & Topics

During each lab meeting, 1) we give one another updates on our research projects and 2) we discuss either current/ongoing work by a group of lab member, an academic paper related to the interests of the lab, or a topic chosen by Sarah.

10/4/21
We'll be hearing from some of the students who completed research internships in the HRI lab during the summer of 2021!

10/11/21
We will form project teams for the Fall quarter.

10/18/21
I (Sarah) will present work conducted by Keziah Naggita, Elsa Athiley, Beza Desta, and myself that was recently submitted to the Human-Robot Interaction Conference titled "Parental Responses to Aggressive Child Behavior towards Robots, Smart Speakers, and Tablets."

Alex Mazursky and Maddie DeVoe will present their work with me that we recently submitted to the Human-Robot Interaction Conference titled "Physical Touch from a Robot Caregiver: Examining Factors that Shape Patient Experience."
